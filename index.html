SHG - Digital Register – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 July 2026

SHG - Digital Register (formerly "Gosthi") is a digital record-keeping app for Self-Help Groups (SHGs). This policy explains how the app handles information.

The short version: all data stays on your phone. the app has no server, requires no account, and does not transmit any data off your device. We (the developers) never see, receive, or store your group's information.

What the app stores on your device

When you use the app, the following is saved only in the app's local storage on your phone:

  • Group details (name, village, district, meeting schedule)
  • Member details (name, photo, phone number, role, joining date)
  • Savings entries, loan records, and repayment records
  • Meeting attendance and voice recordings of meeting decisions

This information is entered by the group's record-keeper and belongs to the group.

What we collect

Nothing. The app makes no network requests with your data. No analytics, no tracking, no advertising identifiers.

Device permissions

  • Camera / Photos — used only to take or choose member photos for the group register. Photos are stored on your device.
  • Microphone — used only to record meeting decisions as voice notes. Recordings are stored on your device.

Consent

Member records should be added with the member's consent. The app records the date consent was given for each member.

Data deletion

Uninstalling the app deletes all data it stored. There is no copy anywhere else.

Important disclaimers

  • This app is a bookkeeping tool. Records in the app are maintained by the group itself and do not constitute official bank records or financial advice.
  • The app never holds, moves, or lends money.
